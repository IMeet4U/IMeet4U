Background & Motivation

Modern people maintain online connections but rarely experience real-world interactions, leading to social isolation and weakened emotional bonds. “MEET U” offers structured and secure anonymous small dinner gatherings to foster deep interpersonal engagement.

Core Features

Anonymous Registration

Reduces social anxiety by revealing user identity on the event day, adding an element of novelty.

Data-Driven Matching

Uses interest questionnaires and algorithms to find like-minded partners.

Real Gatherings

Small dinner events to promote face-to-face interaction and deepen relationships.

Research Objectives

  • Evaluate the effect of anonymous gatherings on reducing social anxiety.
  • Analyze the extent to which data-driven matching enhances user experience.
  • Develop a scalable framework applicable to other social activities.

Business Model

“MEET U” relies primarily on a subscription model ($5–$10 per month), supplemented by advertising and partner commissions, and collaborates with influencers and venue hosts for promotion.

Expected Results

Innovative Platform

Provides a structured and secure social experience.

Increased User Engagement

Boosts user retention and event participation.

80% Event Success Rate

Most registered users are successfully matched and attend gatherings.

User-Friendly Experience

User-friendly interface with simple and intuitive operations.
