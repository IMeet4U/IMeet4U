Background & Motivation

Modern people maintain online connections but rarely experience real-world interactions, leading to social isolation and weakened emotional bonds. “MEET U” offers structured and secure anonymous small dinner gatherings to foster deep interpersonal engagement.

Core Features

Anonymous Registration

Reduces social anxiety by revealing user identity only on event day, adding an element of novelty.

Data-Driven Matching

Leverages interest questionnaires and algorithms to pair like-minded participants.

In-Person Gatherings

Small dinner events that promote face-to-face interaction and meaningful connections.

Research Objectives

  • Evaluate the effect of anonymous gatherings on reducing social anxiety.
  • Analyze how data-driven matching enhances user experience.
  • Develop a scalable framework applicable to other social activities.

Business Model

“MEET U” operates on a subscription model ($5–$10 per month), supplemented by advertising revenue and partner commissions. We collaborate with influencers and venue hosts to expand reach and engagement.
